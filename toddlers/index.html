Patience

Patience is key, your toddler will test you

  • Calm yourself and gather your thoughts before punishment

  • Act out of love, never anger

  • Help your toddler understand what they are doing wrong

  • Deep breaths is always key

Routine

Your child needs to stay productive for good sleep and behavior

  • Create a calendar and stick to it daily.

  • Bedtime should always be the same no matter the day of the week.

Discipline

A toddler needs to be disciplined to show good manners for the future

  • A toddler must be spanked to listen. Otherwise the child will grow up and not obey.
